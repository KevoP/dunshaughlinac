Michael Manning Memorial Dunshaughlin 10km

19.30, Saturday 22nd of June - Dunshaughlin, Co. Meath

Irelands Oldest and Best 10k road race

Dunshaughlin AC are delighted to welcome you to the 2019 Michael Manning Memorial 10km Road Race. This event is proudly supported by Willis Towers Watson and will also be race 5 of this years KIA Race Series events.

Registration and Number pick up at the race HQ in Dunshaughlin Community Centre, and this is also where the post race refreshments and prize giving will take place.

All Finishers will receive our bespoke finishers medal.

Entry Information

Online Registration is available at EventMaster.ie until midnight on the 21st of June.

  • Early Bird Entry April 13th to April 30th. Individual Entry €15.00 plus registration fee
  • Running Group / Running Club April 13th to May 31st. €15.00 plus registration fee. Terms: 15 or more to receive a discount code on application,
  • Race Entry May 1st to June 21st. Individual Entry €20.00 plus registration fee
  • On the Day €20.00

A Technical Race T-Shirt is included with your entry fee with availability guaranteed for the first 800 entries. Sizes to be selected on entry: Small Medium Large x-Large
