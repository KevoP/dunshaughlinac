Training with Dunshaughlin AC

We have various training sessions every week suitble for athletes of all ages and abilities.

Juvenile Training

6:00pm Tuesday on the football pitches (industrial estate)

This session is suitbale for ages 7 to 17

A typical session might involve

  • 10 minutes of jogging to warm up
  • Stretching
  • Running drills
  • A mix of fast and easy running
  • 10 minutes of jogging to cool down

Young athletes can train for fun and fitness or with a view to taking part in events Meath and Leinster

Adult Speedwork

7:30pm Tuesday on the football pitches (industrial estate)

  • 10 minutes of jogging to warm up
  • Stretching
  • Running drills
  • Intervals of fast running of 1 to 4 minutes each followed by a brief period of recovery
  • 10 minutes of jogging to cool down

This session is suitbale for ages 17 and up. Runners of all abilites attend this session. Training is structured so that everyone may run at their own training paces
